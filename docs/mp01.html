NoteProject Description

From the STA 9750 Course Assignment Page:

“You are a data analyst at Netflix, tasked with supporting the Public Relations team. As you may know, Netflix continues to invest heavily in original television and film content, with the goal of producing successful “gourmet cheeseburgers”, i.e., high-quality content that is still accessible to and popular with a global mass-market audience. Accordingly, the PR team wants to put out a series of press releases highlighting recent successes. In this project, you will mine Netflix’s public Top 10 data to identify and quantify these successes.”

longest_films <- GLOBAL_TOP_10 |>

Pushpa 2: The Rule (Reloaded Version) is the longest film to appear in the Netflix global Top 10, with a runtime of 224 minutes (3 hours and 44 minutes). Show Longevity Comparison with Visualization Code 
library(ggplot2)

# Calculate longevity metrics for major markets
longevity_data <- COUNTRY_TOP_10 |>
